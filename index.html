  • You know how to do things

  • Together, you shoud know how to do more then the sum of your know hows

Platform is gucci, "know how" is geico

AI Platforms are bought and sold as if they are the answer to all. Innovation, Optimization, "*"-tion. Your platform is only as good as the people who use it. Your operating procedures ("know how") is your biggest asset. Platforms can fail, they can become your competition, they can be your biggest liability. "Know how" encoded in your people is the only thing that can't be bought or sold. The collective knowledge of how to do things is the only thing that can't be commoditized. Investing and developing "know how" is your insurance policy in a market where everyone has a "platform".
